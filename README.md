# Chocolate Shipment Performance & Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

## üìå Project Overview

This project presents an **enterprise-grade Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze **chocolate shipment performance, supply chain efficiency, and business profitability** across products, regions, teams, and employees.

The dashboard simulates a **real-world supply chain analytics use case**, delivering **executive-level insights** that support strategic decision-making, operational optimization, and performance benchmarking.

The focus of this project is **business intelligence and analytics storytelling**, not raw data exposure.

---

## üéØ Business Problem Statement

Organizations operating in product-based supply chains often struggle to answer questions such as:

- Which products and regions drive the most revenue and profit?
- How are shipment volumes trending over time?
- Which teams and employees contribute most to profitability?
- How does current performance compare year-over-year?
- Where are inefficiencies, losses, or declining trends emerging?

This dashboard addresses these challenges by transforming transactional shipment data into **actionable, decision-ready insights**.

---

## üß† Key Objectives

- Provide a **single source of truth** for shipment and revenue performance  
- Enable **executive-level KPI monitoring**
- Analyze **profitability by product, region, team, and employee**
- Track **shipment volume trends and seasonality**
- Compare **current vs prior year performance**
- Identify **top and bottom performers** to support optimization decisions

---

## üìä Key KPIs Tracked

- Total Revenue  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin (%)  
- Total Shipments  
- Total Boxes Sold  
- Year-over-Year Growth  
- Product-Level & Team-Level Contribution
